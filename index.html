How to Use SoundOfText.co for Generating Text-to-Speech Online for Free

Create voiceovers in multiple global languages and accents with SoundOfText.co for a completely free experience. The official Sound of Text platform seamlessly converts written text into high-quality audio using advanced text-to-speech (TTS) technology, making it a valuable tool for accessibility, productivity, and creativity.

How to Use SoundOfText.co

Visit SoundOfText.co

Step 1: Visit the Official Website

Navigate to the official Sound of Text website: https://soundoftext.co.

Step 2: Enter Your Text

  • Find the input box on the homepage.
  • Type or paste the text you wish to convert into speech. This can range from single words to longer phrases.

Step 3: Select Your Language

  • Use the dropdown menu labeled Language below the text box.
  • Choose your desired language and accent. With support for over 70 languages, you’ll find options like:
    • English (US, UK)
    • Spanish
    • French
    • Indonesian
    • Many more

Step 4: Generate the Audio

  • Click the Submit button.
  • Your text will be processed, and the audio file will appear in the Sounds section.
  • Preview or download the audio file to your device.

Step 5: Download and Use Your Audio

  • In the Sounds section, click the Download button next to your file.
  • Save the audio for use in your projects or share it as needed.
